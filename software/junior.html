We’re looking for junior software engineers to join Co-op Digital and help transform our business by using digital innovation to build products and services that respond to real user needs.

As a software engineer at Co-op Digital you’ll work in one of our multi-disciplinary agile product teams alongside product owners, delivery managers, analysts, researchers and designers. We work together to create solutions that deliver simple applications.

We have a number of products at different stages of maturity. You can read about these in more detail on our blog (digitalblog.coop.co.uk) for more information. You’ll initially join a specific project, but there will be lots of opportunities to move around and keep things interesting. We have a community of digital technologists across digital and the wider Co-op. We come together regularly to share knowledge and learn from each other. The domains for our products vary from funeralcare to healthcare to food and insurance. With everything in between.

We’re primarily building web applications in Python, Javascript and Java, however we’re flexible when it comes to your background. We’re looking for people who are passionate about fulfilling user needs, collaborating with others and building great quality software. We encourage and support a full-stack, polyglot approach.

What you’ll do:

  • bake quality in from the start with the guidance of our expert Quality Analysts

  • work in a collaborative way with engineers and other disciplines by pairing or mob programming
  • work within agile teams to create quick feedback loops and focus on the customer needs
  • work on lots of very different user centred products

What you’ll bring:

  • coding skills: you can create web applications in at least one programming language
  • problem solving
  • a flexible attitude to technology and solid understanding of how the web works
  • the ability to adapt and learn new technologies
  • a desire for continuous improvement
  • a commitment to putting users at the heart of the development process

The process

Our interview process usually takes around 2 weeks from application, sometimes longer. We know this seems a long time, but we think it's worth it. The aim is to ensure this is the right job for you.

  1. Apply. We'll need your CV and have a few questions for you
  2. As part of your application you’ll need to complete an online assessment. It will take you around 12 minutes to complete this test.
  3. Telephone interview (up to 30 mins). We'll have a chat to find out more about you and why you want to work at Co-op
  4. Face to face interview (around 1.5 hours). We'll ask you some technical and behavioural questions.
  5. Final stage interview (half a day). You'll get to see where you'd be sat, who with and what you'd be working on. We'll do some coding together to see your approach to problem solving. Finally you'll meet people you'd be working with who aren't engineers.
